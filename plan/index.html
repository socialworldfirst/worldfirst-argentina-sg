The one dependency

This curve assumes Argentina keeps advancing, which, as title-holders with Messi in record-breaking form, is the likely case, but it's not guaranteed. So flight it adaptively: scale spend up as they progress, and if they exit early, pull the peak budget forward into the harvest immediately (the audience you've already warmed is still yours). Either way, move now, the most valuable two weeks of attention for this prize are happening right now and won't come back.
